About GTS Enterprises

Trust Once,
Services Best

GTS Enterprises was founded in 2017 with a strong vision of providing reliable and efficient Corrosion Protection (CP) solutions across critical infrastructure sectors in India.

With 7+ years of field experience, we maintain 550+ km of underground gas pipeline infrastructure across the Maharashtra region — from Aptibudruk to Borle, and expanding Pan India.

Certified cathodic protection engineers and field technicians
Full compliance with Indian Standards and safety codes
End-to-end project delivery — survey to maintenance
